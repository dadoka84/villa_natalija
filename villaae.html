VILLA EXCLUSIVE A


The owners of VILLA NATALIJA are offering for sale two newly built exclusive villas situated on the unique peaceful location on the Kopar peninsula in the old center of Rogoznica. The location of the villas is ideal for the rest and recreation with a breath taking view on the sea and nearby Marina Frapa, especially during sunrise or sunset. Private pool, sauna, wine-cellar, indoor fireplace, high-tech equipment and the fascinating view present total luxury to customers with style, who put leisure and exclusivity in the first place.
The constructions of the villas started in September 2012. and was completed in June 2013. following the most modern and exclusive standards with the energetic efficiency, using high quality materials, all of them confirmed with adequate official certificates.

Overall description
Both of the villas have a an almost identically lay-out with the only difference in size. The villa A is with it’s size of 472m2 slightly larger then the 417m2 villa B. each of them consists of 5 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, one toilette, large kitchens with adjacent dining rooms, wine cellar, sauna, living rooms with the direct connection with the outdoor space terrace, swimming pool and outside shower. All of the rooms are equipped with the Daikin air-conditioning systems, wireless internet, alarm and satellite TV. On the roofs vacuum solar collectors are used for heating up water.

Both of the villas have two parking places with the installed video security cameras.



Ground floor
Spacious, comfortable full of light, modern designed space ideal for rest and recreation also incorporates sauna and wine-cellar. The large living room offers a view of the sea bathing in the summer sunrays or during the winter months a modern fireplace gives warmth and light to this beautiful space. The living room with it’s large windowpane glass panels connects the total ground space in one large living area with a magnificent terrace, it’s 24m2 swimming pool (massage and heating possibility), and an outside shower. A special part of the living space is a covered intimate space which with an adequate table and chairs can easily accommodate 10 persons so you can enjoy and  entertain guests with various mediterranean delicacies including the first class seafood which you can prepare on the outside grill.







First floor
The ground floor is connected with an inner stairway with the first floor, where the main entrance is located ( due to the configuration of the terrain). On this floor the large glass window pane panels dominate the space and connect it to the terrace with the beautiful view of the sea.
The inside monovolume area contains the kitchen with dining area. On the first floor there are also 3 bedrooms, bathroom with a shower and a toilette.




Second floor
On the second floor there are two spacious bedrooms with the best view on the Marina Frapa from the two largest terraces from which you can enjoy the intricate play of the sun rays and sea waves or just watching sailboats slowly passing by. Both of the bedrooms have private bathrooms and separate closet space. On the opposite side facing the backyard overlooking the entrance and the pine forest there are balconies.
