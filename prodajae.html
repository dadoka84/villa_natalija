Fascinating view on the Marina Frapa, private pool with massage and heating, modern private sauna, indoor fireplace, hi-tech equipment, prevalence of the natural materials for example stone provide that luxury affect available only to selective clientele.



Situated in the middle of the peninsula, formerly an island Kopar, between the cities of Split and Šibenik it is close to many famous natural touristic destinations such as for example the famous natural park-resort of the river Krka, as well as the numerous cultural-historic city monuments dating back to the Roman Empire era. The villas enable you to enjoy the complete privacy for your rest and vacation but also enjoy visiting the famous, natural cultural and historical locations (some of them on the UNESCO list) and also get acquainted with the local population and their customs.


The villas are built of high quality natural materials with a modern design to meet all of the requirements of the 21st century. The modern interior design equipped with the necessary technological equipment gives the final touch to the story of the ideal vacation/living dream come true.
